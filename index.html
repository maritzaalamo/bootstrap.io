Compete

TSA provides rules and guidelines for more than 70 middle school and high school competitions﻿﻿. Students are challenged to use and improve their STEM skills in team and individual events in such areas as: technology; communication; design and engineering; environmental systems; transportation; and manufacturing. All competitions are aligned with STEM standards, leadership skills, and the U.S. Department of Education's National Career Clusters Framework®.
